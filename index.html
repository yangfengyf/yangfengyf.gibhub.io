Bio

Yang Feng is a professor in Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences where she got her phD degree in 2011. She worked in University of Sheffield and Information Sciences Institute, University of Southern California from 2011 to 2014. Now she leads the natural language process group in ICT/CAS and her research interest is natural language processing, mainly focusing on machine translation and dialogue. She was the recipient of the Best Long Paper Award of ACL 2019.
